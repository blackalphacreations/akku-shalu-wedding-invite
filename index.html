Our Story

Sometimes love begins in a single moment… and waits patiently for the right time.

We met for the first time at a bride-to-be celebration that she and her friends had beautifully arranged for my best friend’s fiancée. I had gone there along with my friend, and in the middle of all the laughter and celebrations, I saw her — and that was it for me.

It was love at first sight.

I sent her a request on Instagram, hoping to know her more. Our conversations began, and before I knew it, I had fallen deeply for her. I confessed my feelings, but the timing wasn’t right. Life had different plans, and soon I moved abroad to focus on my future — carrying her in my heart.

Time passed, and we stopped talking.

But some people are never truly forgotten.

After a long gap, I texted her again — not with big expectations, but simply on the occasion of my sister’s wedding. My only intention was to reconnect, to speak to her once more.

That one message changed everything.

The conversations came back, the comfort returned, and so did the feelings that had never really left. This time, when I told her how much she meant to me, it was not just love — it was certainty.

She asked me to speak to our parents.

In one of the most beautiful moments of my life, I asked my mother to call hers, and suddenly everything fell into place as if it had always been meant to happen.

On May 4, 2025, we got engaged.

And now, we are waiting for April 12, 2026 — the day I marry the girl I loved from the very first moment I saw her.

From a chance meeting…
to a message sent just to reconnect…
to a lifetime together ❤️
